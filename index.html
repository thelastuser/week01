Bhūloka is the earth disc, the lowest of the seven "upper regions" of the universe, and the plane of mortal existence.

It has seven islands, arranged concentrically:

  • Jambūdvīpa
  • Śākadvīpa
  • Kuśadvīpa
  • Krauñcadvīpa
  • Śālmalīdvīpa
  • Gomedadvīpa
  • Puṣkaradvīpa
